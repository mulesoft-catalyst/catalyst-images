Asset Contributors: Call to Action

Starting on March 31st, 2021, new asset submissions or updates to Knowledge Hub 1.0 will not be accepted. Instead, please use the new Knowledge Hub Asset Submission Form to submit your asset(s) to Knowledge Hub. This form can also be used for updating existing assets.

Our team will review and post each submission to Knowledge Hub 2.0. If there are questions or issues with the submission, we will contact you directly. If the submission is targeted at external customers and partners, our team will promote the asset to the external Knowledge Hub after review.

As always, if you have any questions or concerns, please contact us using the contact information at the bottom of this newsletter.
